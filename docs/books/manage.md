# Manage a Book

Learn how to manage your book project in the AI/Spec-Driven Book Creation System.

## Book Management Overview

The book management system provides tools to track, edit, and maintain your book project throughout the creation process:

- Project status tracking
- Content organization and structure
- Version control and history
- Collaborator management
- Quality metrics and progress indicators

## Project Tracking

Monitor your book's progress with comprehensive tracking tools:

1. Content completion status
2. Quality validation results
3. Research item resolution
4. Milestone achievements
5. Timeline adherence

## Content Organization

Organize your book content effectively:

- Chapter structure management
- Section hierarchy
- Cross-references and links
- Asset management (images, diagrams, etc.)
- Bibliography and citation tracking

## Quality Assurance

Ensure high-quality output through systematic validation:

- Technical accuracy checks
- Consistency verification
- Style guide compliance
- Readability assessment
- Peer review integration

## Collaboration Tools

Manage team contributions efficiently:

- Role-based permissions
- Review and approval workflows
- Comment and feedback systems
- Change tracking and notifications
- Merge conflict resolution

## Publishing Preparation

Prepare your book for publication:

- Format conversion tools
- Quality assurance checklists
- Export and packaging options
- Distribution preparation
- Post-publication maintenance planning
