## Key Concepts to Focus On

### Physics Simulation
- Physics engines determine how accurately virtual robots behave
- Gravity and collision models affect robot-environment interactions
- Realism limitations exist due to computational constraints

### Visual Rendering
- Unity excels in visual fidelity and human-robot interaction
- Gazebo is preferred for physics accuracy and ROS integration
- Choose the right tool based on your application requirements

### Sensor Simulation
- Realistic noise modeling is crucial for perception algorithm development
- Different sensors have different accuracy and limitation characteristics
- Environmental factors affect sensor performance in simulation
